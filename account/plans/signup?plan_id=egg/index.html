We're no longer taking new accounts, but don't worry!

 

We're no longer taking new accounts, but don't worry! We've built a better version of the Geoloqi technology for you to use! It's called the Geotrigger Service, and it's part of Esri's ArcGIS for Developers platform (which has lots of other great technology for location-savvy developers like you, by the way).

The new and improved Geotrigger Service is currently free and in beta, so go try it out. Just sign up for a free ArcGIS for Developers account and check out our Getting Started guide for the Geotrigger Service, or dive right into the iOS or Android SDKs. When the Geotrigger Service comes out of beta in 2014, we anticipate the pricing should be similar to Geoloqi's plans.

ArcGIS Geotrigger Service

Add location awareness and location-based alerts to native iPhone and Android apps.

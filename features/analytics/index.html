Location-Based Analytics

A complete toolset to analyze and and track location data for your Geoloqi applications

Introduction

Problem: You have no idea how or where people are using your app.

Geoloqi's location-based analytics tools provide rich analytics and visualizations to give you insight into how your users interact with your app.

Use cases

  • How many people are using your app in your stores or at home?
  • How much time are people spending at your stores?
  • What is the peak usage time?
  • What are the most popular cities of your users?

Analytics Features

  • Message conversions - how many push notifications resulted in opening the app?
  • User location history - what other places have your users visited besides your store?
  • Demographics - how much money do your users make? (coming soon)
  • Export custom reports
